Seating

This is a stadium for hosting big events or semi-small events with a seating capacity of 3,500 attendees.

Please contact us for setting limits on seating for your events or if you need assistance visualizing your event setup.

Policies

We strive to make our venue welcoming and inclusive for everyone. Please review our policies to ensure a smooth and enjoyable event:

  • No Smoking: Smoking is prohibited inside the venue and within 50 feet of entrances.
  • Alcohol Service: Alcohol may only be served by licensed and insured bartenders. Proof of licensing is required.
  • Noise Levels: Noise levels must be reduced at 11pm.
  • Decorations: No tape, nails, or adhesives that damage walls or surfaces are allowed. Please consult our team for approved decoration methods.
  • Accessibility: The arena is fully wheelchair accessible. Please let us know if additional accommodations are needed.

Amenities

We offer a wide range of tools to enhance your event experience:

  • Audio/Visual Equipment: High-quality microphones, projectors, and speakers are available for use.
  • Catering Services: We Partnered with local caterers to provide food for staff and attendees. Included with arena rental.
  • Wi-Fi Access: Complimentary high-speed internet is available throughout the arena. Additional fees may apply.
  • Green Room: A private space for event speakers or performers to prepare and relax. Drinks and snacks provided!
  • On-Site Staff: Dedicated staff are available to assist with setup, technical support, logistics, and cleaning.
